Landing on a new page

Challenge: design a landing page


I took this as an opportunity to design a mobile landing page for Stitchology, an e-commerce platform that works to solve the issue of ill-fitting South Asian clothing. We had a desktop landing page already, so this was a matter of following mobile best practices to create a similar experience.


Analyzing

What's the purpose of a landing page?

As the first page of a company's website, this page is crucial in influencing whether a user will stay, or continue browsing for alternatives. The landing page is a fine balance between providing enough information to hook in a user and not overwhelming them with actionable elements or overbearing stimuli.

  • grab attention within about 10 seconds
  • understand the company's purpose or service

Designing

Since we already had a desktop version, I followed best practices and adapted our grid system for mobile.

Grid system:

  • 12 columns
  • margins: 72px
  • gutter: 24px
