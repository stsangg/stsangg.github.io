01.Overview

Background

Northville Rehabilitation Centre is a multidisciplinary clinic located in Markham, ON. This is the second location that the owners have opened, with the first location having been in operation for over 30 years. Though word of mouth is currently their primary source of referrals, Northville would like to expand their marketing efforts and audience demographic.

Details

Role: UX/UI Designer
Timeline: September 2020
Tools Used: Figma, OptimalSort, wix.com
Skills: UX Research, Content Copywriting, Responsive design

Design Opportunity

Over 55% of millennials research their healthcare information online and find it just as “reliable” as their doctor. Compared to baby boomers, they were also twice as likely to have a more holistic approach to health, with 71% citing wellness includes both physical and mental health.

Design Solution

By establishing their online presence and creating an educational and responsive website, Northville hopes to target this younger demographic (specifically 20 to 35 years old). With a revamped website, Northville hopes to establish themselves in the community and align their values with their patients.
