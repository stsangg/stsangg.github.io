Design process

In habit of discovering the why

I turn a mish-mash of ideas, goals, and inspiration into an elegant and tangible product for users. As a problem-solver and systems thinker, I approach projects with the goal to understand. This helps to learn domain knowledge, identify gaps in processes, opportunities for improvement, and most importantly: the why behind user actions. I may not come from a formal design background, but I leverage my Health Sciences degree and past experiences to tackle problems from an evidence-based mindset to create functional and memorable experiences for users.
